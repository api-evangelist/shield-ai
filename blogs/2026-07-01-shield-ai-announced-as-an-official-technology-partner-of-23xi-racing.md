Shield AI is partnering with NASCAR's 23XI Racing to integrate its Hivemind Benchmark analytics tool, which collects and visualizes performance data for driver development. The partnership will be featured on Corey Heim's No. 67 Toyota Camry during the NASCAR Cup Series race at Chicagoland Speedway on July 5.
